Our mission

. . . is to provide innovative and competitive soccer clinics for young girls to grow into strong and confident individuals on and off the field. We want to make a difference by using soccer to empower young girls throughout the world. We believe that sport has the power to change the world, so we plan to make a difference one football at a time.

Alexandra Myers

Born in Russia, raised in USA. Started playing soccer at the age of 3 and quickly fell in love with the game. Aspires to bring people together through universal language we call soccer. #relentless

Hannah McLaughlin

Originally from New Zealand. Been playing soccer since the age of 5. The love and passion for the game has been a big part of her life. Believes that soccer can be used as a platform to make a difference in the world by bringing peace within us which can lead to peace amongst us. #unstoppable

Avril Ramirez

Born in Mexico, moved to USA at 16. Always been a leader on and off the field motivating everyone around her to be a better soccer player and more importantly a better individual. Truly passionate about empowering women soccer, which is the motive to help young girls pursue their dreams through soccer. #fearless
